ANSWER: Yep, a promise is a promise.

You are you and you have your needs and you foresaw them, I do get that. But a reality like this -- how much work kids will require -- is not 100 percent foreseeable. So to enforce it as if it is, at the expense of your wife and kids, is pretty spectacularly selfish and crappy.

So. What are you ready to say about yourself, your child, your wife, your priorities and the variables of life in defending the territory you staked out with this promise?
